Biography

My name is Elena Álvarez Mellado. I’m a Computational Linguistics graduate student at Brandeis University (Massachusetts). I’m interested in linguistics, technology and the intersection between them.

I am also highly involved in linguistic dissemination activities that contribute to making the field of linguistics well-known among the general public. I write a column about language at Spanish national newspaper eldiario.es, I’m member of the editorial board of the linguistics magazine Archiletras and in 2016 I wrote the popular linguistics book Anatomía de la Lengua.
